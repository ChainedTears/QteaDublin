Our Story

Crafted with
Love & Tradition

QTea is a cozy, family-run bubble tea house. We believe that the perfect drink begins with the finest ingredients — from home-brewed tea leaves to top quality syrups.

Our passion lies in fresh-brewed teas and customizable sweetness levels, ensuring each sip is tailored exactly to your taste. Alongside our signature beverages, we serve savory house-made popcorn chicken and other crispy snacks that make every visit a complete experience.
